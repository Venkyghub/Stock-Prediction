Project: Prediction of Stock Price¶

The challenge of this project is to accurately predict the future closing value of a given stock across a given period of time in the future. For this project I will use a Long Short Term Memory networks – usually just called “LSTMs” to predict the closing price of the Bank Niftyusing a dataset of past prices
